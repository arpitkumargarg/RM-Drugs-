Our Story

From Agra to Africa and the Gulf

R.M. Drugs is an established Indian pharmaceutical company headquartered in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, engaged in the marketing, distribution, and export of generic medicines, nutraceuticals, and medical devices across India and international markets.

Built over four and a half decades, our pan-India distribution network reaches pharmacies, hospitals, and clinics across the country. With a team of over 100 dedicated professionals across management, production, and sales, R.M. Drugs is a business built on trust, consistency, and a genuine commitment to the healthcare sector.

All products are supplied through WHO-GMP certified manufacturing facilities across India, ensuring consistent quality, safety, and regulatory compliance in every market we serve.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality and affordable pharmaceutical products to patients and healthcare systems across Asia and Africa — bridging the gap between world-class pharmaceutical quality and competitive pricing, without compromising on safety, efficacy, or compliance.

Our Vision

To become a trusted pan-Asian and pan-African pharmaceutical distribution company recognised for the integrity of our products and the strength of our partnerships. Over the next five years, we aim to establish AIDE as a respected international brand across the Middle East and Africa.

International Brand

About AIDE

Our international export brand

Our generic medicines division operates under an exclusive licence granted by Florence Life Care Private Limited. Florence Life Care authorises R.M. Drugs to manufacture, distribute, market, and export pharmaceutical products under the brand AIDE — a registered trademark of Florence Life Care Pvt. Ltd. — from India to global markets including the Middle East and Africa.

Distribution partners get access to a brand that delivers better shelf appeal and stronger margins over unbranded generics.
